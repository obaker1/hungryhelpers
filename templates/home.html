Hungry Helpers Initiative

Since the COVID-19 pandemic, the distribution of subsidized meals has become an increasing concern due to social distancing and remote learning. Students who are qualified to receive school lunches are unfortunately unable to retrieve them effectively. With some districts suffering from reductions in their meal-service budget, this issue is only worsening. The current lunch program systems are difficult to navigate through, with no simple and flexible process for parents to benefit from.

In an effort to address this concern, Hungry Helpers have designed a web application to provide an organized and efficient system to distribute school lunches to public school students during the pandemic. It will provide a convenient and intuitive way for parents to plan when and where to pick up food and will also help school staff have an efficient structure for food pickups.

Parents can create an account which will save all of their childrenâ€™s necessary information including dietary restrictions, school district, and more. They will then be able to sign up for free or reduced price lunch and choose from available times and locations to pick up their food that is flexible to their needs. This information will then be stored in a way that will be accessible to school staff. Parents will also receive real-time notifications regarding their pickup and any changes that will occur.

The hope of Hungry Helpers is to help parents and schools take advantage of these lunch programs with ease!
